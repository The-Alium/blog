**Starry Steam** - the oldest unit in the community, which specializes in the creation of analytical materials, management of Steam group and developing projects, which ate related to community or depend on gameplay. Also Starry Steam organizes tournaments, calculates games balance, searches for best strategies and interacts with other communities. Starry Steam in the end compiles all gathered information into prepared themed video in multiple languages about this ultra very niche topic.
