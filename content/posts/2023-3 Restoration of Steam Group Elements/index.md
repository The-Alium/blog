At the end of January and the beginning of February, some transformations were made in the Steam group that restored the functionality of previously broken features, thanks to the developers at Valve.

The most important change to note is the cleanup of the Steam chat. All inactive members were removed, with the option to rejoin if they are interested. This was done simply because the member list was outdated; some members had long since passed away, some had left the group, and others had neglected the chat but were still present. For you, as a member of Alium, to rejoin the chat, you need to visit the group page again and click the join button for the chat.

The forums were also cleaned up and updated, with incorrect titles and similar issues being edited. Interestingly, a loophole was found for creating events! You can now leave the Discord server in disgrace! A few people were removed from the group for having unpresentable profiles, falling under community sanctions, and for lack of activity.
