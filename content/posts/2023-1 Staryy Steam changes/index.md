I have returned and made several changes in the group following the completion of the gathering. You can find more detailed results in the FosFor discussion. The Staryy Steam division has been reformed, and the Flashar division is now called the Center.

Soon, Alium Nook will be established as a relevant concept, serving as a map that is connected to the community and responsive to ongoing events within it. There have been new changes in group moderation, including the addition of new positions such as reviewer and community curator. Descriptions of other roles have been simplified, and a role compatibility table has been created.

A separate forum will be introduced with guides in various languages, coordinating people from other countries to work with Alium. This forum will also host content from other forums in different languages. The concept of reports in announcements will be introduced. The community sanction prohibiting users who supported the war from being members of Alium is transitioning from de jure to de facto. Additionally, I have decided to conduct a small cleanup of members with unpresentable profiles, specifically those with inappropriate usernames.
