After several days of well-deserved rest, we are returning to local project work - the Plan for the Extensive Restoration and Publication of Archived Maps for Counter-Strike: Source.

For this plan, we primarily need dedicated players who can commit a few hours of focused gameplay every weekend over the next few months. They are needed to write map reviews and to play 50 rounds to calculate the win rate of teams in the Zombie Plague mode.

All gatherings regarding this matter are conducted in Discord, where the chat for regular participants of the plan is also located.
