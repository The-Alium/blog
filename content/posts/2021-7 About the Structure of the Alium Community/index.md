Yesterday, a video was released that was entirely dedicated to the structure of Alium. If you previously had any confusion about the internal workings of the group, you can watch this video to gain a better understanding of the overall picture.

Changes are already being observed due to a significant influx of users: Alium gatherings will now take place in Discord to prevent the server from crashing when everyone tries to access the podium at once.

The nomination for the executor of SORIP is once again open. If you have any experience in reporting publications within the community, please contact Erick_Maksimets for an explanation of the current situation regarding the plan.
