With the issuance of this announcement, the concept of the Grata System officially comes into effect within the group.

The Grata System serves as an alternative to the list of undesirable users, rendering the previous resolution for community cleanup invalid from this moment forward.

Friendship with Non Grata individuals cannot be used as a reason for exclusion from the group; however, it may serve as a basis for imposing sanctions against those individuals. Additionally, friendship with Non Grata individuals can be a reason for denying membership in the group if the user is not part of the community.

A point regarding subjectivism from the user has been added to the loyalty points list. For positive activities related to Grata individuals, you can receive additional approval from the community.
