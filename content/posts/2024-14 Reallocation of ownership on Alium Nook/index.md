On October 31, 2024, a radically new version of Alium Nook has been released by DefaultOS, which had been in development for about a year.

DefaultOS has fundamentally changed the familiar Alium Nook while retaining the original map's essence. The player spawn has been redesigned, and the lift that used to lead to the parking lot in older versions has made a return. The main hall is as new as it is old in its appearance. The eagle statue now looks more professionally crafted. Many familiar rooms, such as the bar, reception, auditorium, conference room, lounge, etc., have been completely reimagined. The cinema has also returned. Notably, the courtroom stands out as a stunning redesign of the old hall, featuring an excellent appearance and new functionalities.

The new version of Alium Nook by DefaultOS offers a fresh perspective on the beloved map, accompanied by high-quality design and outstanding execution. His version will exist separately in the workshop under his authorship. Our version of Alium Nook will receive an update utilizing his developments.
