An update has been made to Alium Nook aimed at updating positions within the community, with references left to PORiPAK, the Green Alium, and the activities of Sor_Ge, along with many minor changes and proper optimization.

The moderator concept has been expanded to include the roles of Ombudsman for Grata, Auditor, and Prosecutor of the Workshop, which will fully open after the necessary review of cases in the Lakur Court.

Additionally, I (Erick_Maksimets) created a poster reflecting the state of affairs in Alium as of November 2021. Further changes are expected in 2022.
