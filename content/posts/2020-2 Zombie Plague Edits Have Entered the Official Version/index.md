Finally, after several months of development in collaboration with The Fire Fuchs, balance edits, translations into Ukrainian and Russian, various features, and bug fixes have made their way into the official Steam Workshop version of Zombie Plague.

All these enhancements to the game mode were previously exclusive to the local servers of the Alium community, but now, for the game mode to function properly, there is no need to download any additional fixes. Everything is already available in the Steam workshop.

A fourth version of the balance and an analysis of the weapon characteristics from Half-Life 2 have been specifically calculated for the game mode. Thank you all for your effectiveness in testing, or perhaps for your lack of effectiveness.
