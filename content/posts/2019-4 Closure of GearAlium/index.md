Good day, today I am here to share some rather neutral news. The server rental period has come to an end, and there is no desire to continue hosting the server. In principle, I could have asked the system administrator to extend the rental for another month or more, but in recent days, the online player count has dropped from an average of eight to four players.

I am not sure when we will be able to resume operations, but I should inform you that most of the server files have been saved. There is no longer a desire to maintain the server, so we are moving into "underground" mode.

These were truly good days; thank you all for playing. We managed to hold on without donations, didn't we?
