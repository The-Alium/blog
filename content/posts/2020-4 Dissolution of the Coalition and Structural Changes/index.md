After observing the situation with the playable online community in the Coalition of Alium Players, I realized that it holds no value and deserves to be dissolved.

As a result, the structure of the community will undergo numerous changes. The group is now open to all individuals who have not been subjected to sanctions from previous servers. Old players will not receive any privileges from the group.

The Coalition of Alium Players has been removed, and a chat called The Alium has been created. The group will no longer position itself as a server group, and players from the Coalition have lost their voting rights. Additionally, I have updated the group's description to reflect a more current perspective.
